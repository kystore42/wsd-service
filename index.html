FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions.

How long does one session last?

+

Each individual session lasts 45-60 minutes, depending on the child's needs and age. I create a comfortable pace for each student.

What is the cost of services?

+

The cost depends on the type of lessons (individual/group) and frequency. Contact me for detailed information about current rates and packages.

Do you offer online lessons?

+

Yes! I conduct both in-person and online lessons via Zoom/Google Meet. Online format is especially convenient for families with busy schedules.

How quickly will we see results?

+

Each child is unique. Usually, the first improvements are noticeable after 4-6 weeks of regular lessons. I provide progress reports to parents every month.
